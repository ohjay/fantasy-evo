About
This is a website tailored to manage SSBM fantasy drafts for the Evolution 2015 Championship Series, which will be held during July 17-19 in Las Vegas. The concept is simple: form a league with your friends, in which each person chooses a certain number of players for their draft. At the end of the tournament, everyone will receive points based on how well their players performed at EVO â€“ the person with the highest number of points wins!

Note: at the moment, this website only supports fantasy leagues for the SSBM tournament at EVO 2015.
