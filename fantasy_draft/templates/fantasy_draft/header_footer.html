About

This is a website tailored to manage Super Smash Bros: Melee fantasy drafts and leagues for the duration of major tournament seasons. The concept is simple: form a league with your friends, in which each person will choose a certain number of players for his/her draft. At the end of each tournament, everyone will receive points based on how well their players performed – come the season's finish, the person with the highest number of points wins!

For more details about the rules behind Fantasy SSBM (plus the tournament season listings), see here.
