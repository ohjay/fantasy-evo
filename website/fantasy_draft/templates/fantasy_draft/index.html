About

This is a website tailored to manage Super Smash Bros: Melee fantasy drafts and leagues for the duration of major tournament seasons. The concept is simple: form a league with your friends and choose 1-10 players for your draft. At the end of each tournament, everyone will receive points based on how well their players performed. Come the season's finish, the person with the highest number of points wins!

For further details (including the tournament season listings), take a look at the Information page.
